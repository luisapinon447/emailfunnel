Build Your Own Crossbow?

Dear Survivalist,

There are some hard truths we must face for when TSHTF...

First: Bullets WILL run out...

Second: You MUST be able to defend yourself...

And Third: Knives won't always be the best answer...

What is the best solution?

A Crossbow - they are silent, can defend you from a distance and you can build more ammo easily.

Not to mention they are great for hunting large and small game!

And best of all you can always build one yourself. Something easier said than done when it comes to guns.
